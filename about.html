About Baal Sheelah

Welcome to Baal Sheelah

Baal Sheelah is a dedicated resource for Baalei Teshuva (those returning to Judaism) and Giurim (those converting to Judaism). Our mission is to provide comprehensive support, guidance, and educational materials to help individuals on their journey towards embracing Jewish faith and practice.

Mission

Baal Sheelah, we understand the unique challenges and questions that arise during the spiritual journey of returning or converting to Judaism. Our mission is to create a supportive community that fosters growth, learning, and connection to Jewish traditions and values.

What We Offer

  • Educational Resources: Access a wealth of articles, books recomendations, and religious texts that cover various aspects of Jewish life, including Torah study, mitzvot (commandments), and Jewish history.
  • Guidance and Support: Our support network is here to answer your questions and provide personalized guidance.
  • Community Engagement: Participate in our Facebook community, discussion groups, and local events to build meaningful connections with others who share your journey.
  • Practical Information: Find practical advice on navigating Jewish holidays, kosher food, prayer practices, and family life according to Jewish law and tradition.
  • Drawings: Drawings, drawings and more drawings because sometimes worlds are not enough

Join Our Community

Whether you are just starting your journey or have been traveling this path for some time, Baal Sheelah is here to support you every step of the way. Join our community to learn, grow, and connect with others who are passionate about embracing Jewish life. Facebook
